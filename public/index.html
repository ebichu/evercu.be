Evercube

A small and energy-efficient storage server, designed for 24/7-operation — right in your living room.
This easy-to-assemble DIY kit was inspired by the famous Backblaze Storage Pod. While not offering quite as much storage capacity, it will run almost silently and draw very little power.

Facts

dimensions 150 x 150 x 150 mm
material brushed stainless steel, 2 mm
processor Marvell Sheeva, single core, 1.2 Ghz
memory 512 MB, DDR2 400 Mhz
disks 5 x SATA2, 2.5"
max. capacity 5 TB
power 20 W (5 VDC, 4 A)
cooling ultra-silent fan, 140 mm

FAQ

What is included in the kit?
Everything except harddisks.
Do I need soldering skills to build the Evercube?
No, the only things you need to finish this project are screwdrivers — and a little patience.
When is the kit available?
The first kits will be ready for delivery in October 2011.
